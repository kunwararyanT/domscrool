About Domscrool

Domscrool is a satirical web application designed to simulate the modern dopamine loop of infinite scrolling and late-night impulse shopping. We capture the aesthetic rush of adding viral products to a cart without any of the financial regret.

How It Works

There are no real transactions on this site. The products, user handles, view counts, and checkouts are entirely simulated for entertainment and digital behavioral commentary. Enjoy the serotonin spike of a fake 20% discount code.

Operation & Legal Info
